Maddy Daddy's BBQ

Menu

Want a taste of the South here in Provo? Well these two Georgia Boys have your back!

Jackson and Lindy Madsen are two eating experts that love to throw meat on the smoker! Relocated from Atlanta, GA, these two dudes are here in Utah County serving up their favorite southern delicacies for low prices and a limited time each weekend. Better act quick!

Each weekend, the boys will be serving up a different kind of BBQ, so make sure you call ahead or check out the monthly roatation below!





Take a look at our rotating menu!

  1. Pulled Pork Week
    • classic smoked pork shoulder, served as a plate combo or sandwich combo
    • cole slaw, pickles, and potato salad on the side
  2. Beef Brisket Week
    • Rich, smokey Beef Brisket, order a serving of the lean flat or the fatty point
    • Get a side of texas toast, charro beans, and collard greens
  3. Chicken Week
    • Juicy smoked chicken thighs and wings
    • pair them with our mac salad and baked beans
  4. St. Louis cut Pork Ribs Week
    • Get a 3-rib slab of our saucy, pull-apart ribs
    • grab some corn bread, mac n cheese, and brunswick stew to go with your order
